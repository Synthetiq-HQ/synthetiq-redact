# Responsible Use

Synthetiq Redact assists human redaction review. Councils remain responsible for final disclosure decisions.

## Required Use Pattern

- Upload documents only into council-approved infrastructure.
- Review proposed redactions before release.
- Treat handwriting, low OCR confidence, safeguarding, children, health, legal, and social-care material as mandatory review.
- Keep audit evidence with the release pack.
- Use synthetic or consented fake data for demos and public testing.

## Do Not Use For

- Fully automatic legal disclosure decisions.
- Unreviewed release of FOI, SAR, EIR, legal, safeguarding, health, or social-care material.
- Sending real council documents to external AI services without council approval.
- Public demos using real personal data.
- Claims of guaranteed GDPR, FOI, SAR, or EIR compliance.

## Public Statement

Synthetiq Redact assists redaction review. It does not replace council staff, DPOs, legal review, or final disclosure decision making.
